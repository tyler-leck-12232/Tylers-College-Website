Planet Base

My Online Game Server

Planet Base is a open world based server where you can explore the vast majority of planets around the universe. I am currently working on developing the dark side of the moon. There will be many things to-do such as explore and defeat enimes in the vast areas of the moon. You can also fight people in 1 on 1 battles called Spars. In the future after I make it our server will be the first to have zero gravity spars. I am also in the midst of making a currency system so that players will be able to earn money trade for items and trade for other players items. Players will also be able to battle other players over goals like power plants and bases where you can make money. You can also work at businesses to make money while stocking up the place so others can buy things.                                                 

When I started out I knew very little about game programming now after looking back I can pick up pretty much any programming language very quickly. I have learned all the basics of javscript and unity just by developing on the engine. I also understand the more advanced side of the development such as making menus and player to player interaction with a server. I also wrote/expanded upon certain pre-developed tools for the engine such as the map renderer written in javascript and the map generator written in python. These tools I used to randomly generate the moon map based on a few pre-made levels. I repared the map renderer because windows updates ended up breaking it. Also some of the tools in the client such as the animation editor had many features that were either broken or missing. I also made dark themes for many of the guis in game since they had this blue theme on them by default. I love making this game because I feel it is something that I will continue on in the future and continue expanding my programming knowlege.
